Rule of thumb

      

        

          
Date-only

          
Birthdays, report days, policy dates, due dates measured in calendar days.

          
"renewalDate": "2026-01-12"
// Keep as string, or Temporal.PlainDate

        

        

          
Instant

          
Created-at, paid-at, log timestamps, moments on a global timeline.

          
"createdAt": "2026-01-12T14:30:00Z"
// Parse as Date/Instant

        

        

          
Risky

          
Treating a date-only field as a JS Date instant and formatting it locally.

          
new Date("2026-01-12")
// May display Jan 11 west of UTC
